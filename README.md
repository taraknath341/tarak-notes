# 📝 Tarak Notes

> Your notes, everywhere.

**Tarak Notes** is a free, open-source cloud notepad built by **Tarak Program** (non-profit).  
Write, organise, translate, and enhance your notes with built-in AI — all in one place.


## ✨ Features

- ☁️ **Cloud-first Storage**  
  Notes are saved instantly and synced across all your devices.

- 🤖 **AI-powered Writing**  
  Expand, summarise, rewrite, or brainstorm directly inside your notes.

- 🌍 **Instant Translation**  
  Translate text into 50+ languages without leaving the editor.

- 📂 **Smart Organisation**  
  Use folders, tags, and full-text search to find anything quickly.

- 🔒 **Privacy First**  
  Your notes belong to you. No tracking, no selling data.

- 💡 **Open Source**  
  Transparent and community-driven under the MIT License.
